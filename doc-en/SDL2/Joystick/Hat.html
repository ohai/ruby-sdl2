Overview

This module provides constants of joysticks's hat positions used by SDL2::Joystick class. The position of the hat is represented by OR'd bits of RIGHT, LEFT, UP, DOWN. This means the center position (CENTERED) is represeted by 0 and the left up position LEFTUP is represeted by (LEFT|UP).
